Surat City

Surat is a major textile hub in Gujarat.It is located at south of the Gujarat.It is also called the Diamond city.Some of the major tourist places are:

    Gopi Talav
    Diamond Borse
    Dumas Beach
    Sneh Rashmi Botanical Garden
    Hazira
    Suvalli Beach
    Swaminarayan Gurukul
    Galteshwar
    Olpad
    Surat Fort
